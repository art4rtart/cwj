Introduce.

Video games have been my passion for many years now. I enjoy playing them, making them or just watching people play them. For now, I have graduated from college as a student of Game Engineering and I have been learning how to create games for the past 5+ years, mostly using Unity 3D. Main Project.

'Lab25' synopsis is a vaccine lab that already been over 100 days since the outbreak of the virus. The Director of the Lab closed Zone 25, the site of the spill, and so employees were forcibly quarantined, and contact with the outside was finally cut off from developing vaccines in other zones. Over time, employees show signs of bloodshot eyes and aggressive tendencies, an early sign of infection, and are now mostly infected. The protagonist tells the story of escaping to survive in isolation in a vaccine room.
